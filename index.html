245 - Corrosion Equipment
Step 01
Identify the Condition

Use Code 245, Corrosion – Equipment, when there is evidence of severe external corrosion on any pole-mounted electrical equipment.

Step 02
Emergency Response

For emergency conditions, immediately notify the electric construction supervisor and stand by until the supervisor is on-site. Do not leave the area unattended.

Step 03
Follow-Up Reference

Refer to Standard Practice 122 for detailed follow-up requirements and documentation procedures after the initial response.
